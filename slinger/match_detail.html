RULES

General Community Policies

1. You must be over 18 years of age to join. (Minors found to be in county without prior authorization will receive permanent bans. Players found to be knowingly harboring minors in county without notifying staff will be subject to disciplinary action.)

2. All Govt. (Administrators) have the right to resolve any situation.

3. Sexist, racist, homophobic or otherwise discriminatory statements or actions are prohibited. This is a ZERO TOLERANCE policy.

4. Forced RPs or IC or OOC situations that cause discomfort to another player are not allowed. If a member of the community asks you to stop, or if you are uncomfortable participating in the scene, you must respect that request.

5. Any roleplay of a sexual nature without the consent of both players is prohibited.

6. The main language of the server is English.

7. When faced with another player breaking server rules:

1.Stay in character and finish the scene. (Do not break character by trying to “rule-splain”.)

2.Take a video clip of the incident (2 minutes leading up to the rule break and 2 minutes after)

3.Open a ticket and provide the clip(s) to staff to review and handle

Discord Rules

1.ONLY information found in the “IN COUNTY RP” channel category in Discord may be used in county.

2. Your Slinger Valley Discord profile must match at least one of your character names.

3. Disrespecting the community can lead to a ban.

4. DO NOT send private messages directly to staff or business owners. Make a ticket and we will help you further.

5. When submitting a ticket:

1.Player reports/complaints require time and a description of the incidents and people involved. In most situations, video footage of the incident is required, including sound. (Medal.tv recommended)

2. Be respectful and patient with all tickets.

Gameplay Rules

The following subsections contain the Gameplay Rules for Slinger Valley Roleplay

Recording Software

Recording software is required, unless express permission is granted by Staff.

Character Creation

1. Character Creation: Characters must follow the following rules:

1. Characters must be over 18 years of age unless authorized by staff.

2.Use only realistic names.

3. Staff reserves the right to remove characters whose names are offensive, memes, or otherwise inappropriate.

Mindsharing

1. Mind Sharing: Mind sharing is the use of knowledge known to other characters you play (often referred to as cousins, other pairs of boots, altos, etc.) onto another character. This is a form of power gaming and is EXPRESSLY prohibited. This includes a character telling someone else to say something to one of your characters.

Voluntary Name Changes

A voluntary name change must be approved by an official application.
